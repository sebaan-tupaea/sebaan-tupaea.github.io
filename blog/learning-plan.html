    Long term, I want to be a DevOps engineer—getting into automating workflows, solving problems, and keeping the IT scene on flawless. As a student, I am hungry to learn, level up, and bring some real drive to the tech game, making sure everything runs smoothly.

  2. A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

    3. I have a passion for learning and exploring new ideas, which keeps me curious and motivated to dive deeper. I am pretty persistent, tackling tough problems until I solve them, and I am adaptable, able to switch up my learning approach to fit different materials and teaching styles. These strengths help me grow and stay engaged with challenging topics.

    However, time management is where I struggle the most. Juggling multiple complex subjects and other responsibilities can be overwhelming, and I sometimes fall into procrastination or end up rushing my work at the last minute. My perfectionism does not help either, as it slows me down, making it hard to move on until everything feels just right. Improving how I balance these elements is something I really need to work on.

  3. What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

    4. At Dev Academy, I am aiming to level up my time management skills so I can keep everything organised and meet deadlines with ease. I am also looking to boost my confidence, making me more assertive and ready to shine in every challenge. Plus, I want to get better at asking for help when I need it, so I can get the support I need to overcome obstacles and make the most of my learning journey.
